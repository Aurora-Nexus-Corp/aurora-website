Our Story

Built by Integrators, for Integrators

Aurora Nexus was founded by security industry veterans who experienced firsthand the lack of purpose-built software for integrators. We saw an industry forced to cobble together generic tools that didn't understand the unique workflows of security integration.

So we set out to change that. We're building a suite of software products designed from the ground up to solve the real challenges faced by security integration companies of all sizes.

Our Mission

Empowering Security Integrators to Thrive

We believe security integrators deserve software built specifically for their industry. Our mission is to create tools that make running your business easier, so you can focus on what matters most: protecting people and property.
